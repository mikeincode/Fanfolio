# Fanfolio Beta Testing Guide

## What is Fanfolio?

Fanfolio is a **fake-money educational sports market simulator**. You use LuckyCoin — simulated in-app currency — to buy and sell fictional sports-themed assets, learn how financial markets work, and track your simulated portfolio performance.

**LuckyCoin has no cash value.** Fanfolio does not involve real money, real gambling, real deposits, real withdrawals, real prizes, real betting, real odds, real parlays, or any form of real financial activity. It is a learning tool.

---

## Beta Tester Checklist

Work through each step and use the **Send Beta Feedback** button (in Settings or Profile) to report anything that feels broken, confusing, or off.

1. **Finish onboarding** — complete the intro flow and reach the Home screen
2. **Claim LuckyCoin** — tap the daily claim card on the Home screen
3. **Watch one asset** — go to Market, find an asset, and add it to your Watchlist
4. **Buy one asset** — use LuckyCoin to purchase at least one asset
5. **Review Today's Market Pulse** — read the market event card on the Home screen
6. **Check Portfolio** — visit the Portfolio tab and review your holdings
7. **Use the Scanner** — open the Scanner tab and try a preset filter
8. **Read one Learn lesson** — open the Learn tab and read any lesson to completion
9. **Check Ranks / Profile** — visit the Profile screen and view your rank and XP
10. **Send feedback** — use "Send Beta Feedback" in Settings or Profile

---

## What Feedback to Send

Please report anything in the following categories:

- **Broken buttons or taps that do nothing**
- **Confusing wording** — anything that sounds like real money, real gambling, or real investing
- **Layout issues** — text that overflows, elements that overlap, or screens that feel cramped
- **Fun parts** — what felt engaging or satisfying
- **Pointless parts** — features that felt confusing or added no value
- **Gambling-adjacent language** — anything that feels too much like real betting, odds, parlays, or real money trading
- **Anything else** — weird edge cases, crashes, or unexpected behavior

## Known Beta Limitations

| Area | Status |
|------|--------|
| Market data | All simulated — not real sports data |
| Sports assets | Generic names (no real team/player/league branding) |
| Cloud save | Optional — local save always works |
| Build | Beta (beta-rc1) — expect rough edges |
| Leaderboard | Simulated rankings only |
| Events | Market events are pre-written simulations |

---

## Legal

LuckyCoin has no cash value and cannot be exchanged for real money, prizes, or goods.

Fanfolio does not involve real money, gambling, deposits, withdrawals, brokerage services, sportsbook odds, parlays, or cash prizes of any kind.

Cloud save stores simulated game progress only. No payment or financial account data is collected or stored.
